I decided to make the navbar the same red and white as the nintendo logo, even finding out the exact code of the color red they use in their logo and copying it in the red for my navbar. I did the same for the blue hover animation, the hover color being the same color as their blue joycon on the nintendo switch. I made the text black on click because the grey was too light but the black was also prevalent upon looking at the consolde. I like the sleek design of the red, blue and black elements all fit together on the Switch so that's why I chose this product and brand to base my navbar off of.
